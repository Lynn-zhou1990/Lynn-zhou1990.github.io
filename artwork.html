ARTWORK

Charcoal drawing

This is a random created of art work that with an oil palette and different colors of oil paints and acrylic. The white acrylic paint is scraped and daubed on the painting board at will and boldly with the oil painting scraper to create a uneven texture feeling. It should not be deliberately released, the body and mind should be competely released together, and it should be created boldly and random without thinking too much. Then, sprinkle different colors of oil paint on the texture surface and let it flow freely and naturally along the texture surface. The creative process put the emotion of open and free into my activity.
